Aiden Oliver

Web Developer & Open Source Contributor

Background

Born in London, raised in California. Developed a knack for building and design since I was 6 years old, which eventually landed my interest in web development. Now at the age of 20, I have committed most of my free time to web development, learning new libraries, languages and design. Curiosity has always been my strong suit and I believe that is what propels my understanding of development and design.

When creating a website or web app I keep in mind how responsive it is and test the site in a wide range of devices. As I develop and maintain a project I ensure that it is scalable and easy to understand so you can enhance a website as your company grows. First impressions are important, that's why I believe the user experience is the most important part to get right.

Skills

As of right now I specialize in Front-end development but I am committed to learning all the layers of Full-stack development and more. I've also learned during many projects that debugging and the troubleshooting process comes with the territory of the development life cycle and is quite important.

Web Development

I have a broad toolset when it comes to the front-end build process. I use HTML/CSS, Bootstrap (Responsive HTML/CSS), and JavaScript(vanilla, Node, Angular, jQuery, etc). To speed up the development process I use version control and task runners such as Git, Gulp, Grunt, NPM, Github, and CSS preprocessors like Less and Sass. I can quickly and effectively join any team using continuous-integration with methods and tools such as Git, Agile/Scrum, and Jenkins. I'm also just as good working independently on smaller projects too.
